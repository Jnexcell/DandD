# 🎲 Build Your Character — The Chooser

**New to D&D? Welcome — you don't need to know the rules.** Just **circle one option** in each step below
and pick a name. Each choice is explained in plain language. When you're done, hand this back and we'll
turn your circles into a ready-to-play character sheet, with everything your character can do spelled out.

> Your character = **Race** (what you *are*) + **Class** (what you *do*) + **Background** (where you *came
> from*) + **Ability Scores** (your raw talent) + a **personality**. Circle one in each step.
> Jargon note: **AC** = Armor Class (how hard you are to hit) · **HP** = Hit Points (your health) ·
> a **modifier** is the +/− number you add to a 20-sided die (d20) roll.

---

## STEP 1 — Circle a RACE  ◯

*Your race gives small ability boosts, a speed, and a few always-on traits. If a race has **subraces**,
circle one of those too.*

- **◯ Dwarf** — *Con +2 · Medium · speed 25 (heavy armor doesn't slow you).* Stout, tough, traditional.
  See in the dark (Darkvision 60 ft), shrug off poison (Dwarven Resilience), and sense stonework.
  **Subrace:** ◯ *Hill* (+Wis 1, **extra durable** — +1 HP every level) · ◯ *Mountain* (+Str 2, can wear
  light/medium armor). **Pick if you want:** to be sturdy and hard to kill.

- **◯ Elf** — *Dex +2 · Medium · speed 30.* Graceful, perceptive, long-lived. Darkvision 60, can't be
  magically put to sleep and resist being charmed (Fey Ancestry), sharp eyes (proficient in Perception),
  and meditate instead of sleeping (Trance). **Subrace:** ◯ *High* (+Int 1, a free wizard cantrip, extra
  language) · ◯ *Wood* (+Wis 1, **speed 35**, hide in nature — Mask of the Wild) · ◯ *Drow / Dark Elf*
  (+Cha 1, Darkvision **120**, innate magic, but sunlight is a problem). **Pick if you want:** nimble and
  perceptive.

- **◯ Halfling** — *Dex +2 · Small · speed 25.* Small, lucky, plucky. Reroll natural 1s (Lucky), hard to
  frighten (Brave), and can move through bigger creatures' space. **Subrace:** ◯ *Lightfoot* (+Cha 1,
  naturally stealthy — hide behind bigger creatures) · ◯ *Stout* (+Con 1, poison-resistant). **Pick if you
  want:** a lucky underdog who's hard to pin down.

- **◯ Human** — *+1 to all six abilities · Medium · speed 30.* The flexible all-rounder — good at any class,
  plus a bonus language. **◯ Variant Human option:** instead, +1 to **two** abilities of your choice, one
  skill, and **a feat** (a special talent) right away. **Pick if you want:** flexibility, or an early feat.

- **◯ Dragonborn** — *Str +2, Cha +1 · Medium · speed 30.* Proud dragon-blooded warriors. Pick a dragon
  type (Draconic Ancestry); you get a **Breath Weapon** (a cone/line of elemental damage you can use in a
  fight) and **resistance** to that damage type. *(No darkvision.)* **Pick if you want:** a strong warrior
  with a built-in ranged blast.

- **◯ Gnome** — *Int +2 · Small · speed 25.* Tiny, clever, curious. Darkvision 60 and a strong mind against
  magic (Gnome Cunning — advantage on mental saves vs. spells). **Subrace:** ◯ *Forest* (+Dex 1, the
  *minor illusion* cantrip, talk to small animals) · ◯ *Rock* (+Con 1, build tiny clockwork gadgets).
  **Pick if you want:** a brainy, magical small character.

- **◯ Half-Elf** — *Cha +2 and +1 to two others · Medium · speed 30.* Charming and adaptable. Darkvision 60,
  resist charm (Fey Ancestry), and **proficiency in two skills of your choice** (Skill Versatility).
  **Pick if you want:** a charismatic, do-a-bit-of-everything character (great "face").

- **◯ Half-Orc** — *Str +2, Con +1 · Medium · speed 30.* Powerful and relentless. Darkvision 60, drop to
  **1 HP instead of 0** once per rest (Relentless Endurance), hit harder on critical hits (Savage Attacks),
  and proficient in Intimidation. **Pick if you want:** a tough frontline brawler who won't go down.

- **◯ Tiefling** — *Int +1, Cha +2 · Medium · speed 30.* Fiendish heritage, magnetic and mysterious.
  Darkvision 60, **resistance to fire**, and innate magic (the *thaumaturgy* cantrip now; more later).
  **Pick if you want:** a charismatic outsider with a spark of magic.

## STEP 2 — Circle a CLASS  ◯

*Your class is what your character actually does. "Hit Die" hints at toughness (d12 = very tough … d6 =
fragile). A few classes make an extra choice at level 1 — that's marked **(choose now)** and we'll do it
together.*

- **◯ Barbarian** — *Front-line bruiser · d12 (toughest) · Strength.* You **Rage**: bonus damage and you
  take **half** damage from most weapons while raging. Reckless, durable, simple to play. **Pick if you
  want:** to soak hits and smash things. *(Level 1: Rage, tough unarmored defense.)*

- **◯ Bard** — *Versatile support & "face" · d8 · Charisma · full spellcaster.* You **inspire** allies
  (hand out bonus dice), talk your way through problems, and cast a flexible spell list. **Pick if you
  want:** magic + social skills + a bit of everything. *(Level 1: spells, Bardic Inspiration.)*

- **◯ Cleric** — *Divine healer/leader in armor · d8 · Wisdom · full spellcaster.* You **heal and buff**
  the party, blast and control with divine magic, command undead, and can wear armor and hold the line.
  **Pick if you want:** to keep everyone alive with flexible magic. *(Level 1: spells + **Divine Domain
  (choose now)** — e.g. Life=healing, War/Tempest=armored fighter, Light=fire & radiance.)*

- **◯ Druid** — *Nature caster & shapeshifter · d8 · Wisdom · full spellcaster.* You control the field with
  nature magic, heal, and soon **Wild Shape** into animals. **Pick if you want:** a nature theme with lots
  of versatility. *(Level 1: spells, Druidic; shapeshifting comes at level 2.)*

- **◯ Fighter** — *Weapon master, most flexible warrior · d10 (very tough) · Strength or Dexterity.* You
  reliably hit hard, get a burst of extra actions (Action Surge later), and **self-heal** with Second Wind.
  **Pick if you want:** straightforward, deadly, customizable combat. *(Level 1: **Fighting Style (choose
  now)** — Archery/Dueling/Defense/etc. — and Second Wind.)*

- **◯ Monk** — *Mobile martial artist · d8 · Dexterity & Wisdom.* Fast and unarmored, you throw a flurry of
  unarmed strikes and dart around the battlefield (Ki powers from level 2). **Pick if you want:** an agile
  striker who needs no weapons or armor. *(Level 1: Martial Arts, unarmored defense.)*

- **◯ Paladin** — *Holy warrior · d10 (tough) · Strength & Charisma · half spellcaster.* You **Smite** foes
  with bursts of radiant damage, protect allies with auras, **Lay on Hands** to heal, and swear a sacred
  Oath. **Pick if you want:** a durable hero who deals damage *and* supports. *(Level 1: Divine Sense, Lay
  on Hands; spells & Oath come at 2–3.)*

- **◯ Ranger** — *Wilderness skirmisher · d10 (tough) · Dexterity & Wisdom · half spellcaster.* You track
  and hunt, fight well at range or with two weapons, and pick up light nature magic. **Pick if you want:**
  an outdoorsy striker with skills and a little magic. *(Level 1: Favored Enemy, Natural Explorer.)*

- **◯ Rogue** — *Skill & stealth expert · d8 · Dexterity.* You deal big burst damage with **Sneak Attack**,
  dart in and out (Cunning Action later), and have the best skills in the game — **Expertise** doubles your
  bonus in two of them. **Pick if you want:** sneaky, skillful, big single hits. *(Level 1: Sneak Attack,
  Expertise, Thieves' Cant; you pick **4** skills.)*

- **◯ Sorcerer** — *Innate blaster · d6 (fragile) · Charisma · full spellcaster.* Magic in your blood: a
  focused spell list, plus **Metamagic** (from level 3) to twist spells on the fly. **Pick if you want:**
  flashy, dramatic magic. *(Level 1: spells + **Sorcerous Origin (choose now)** — e.g. Draconic, Wild
  Magic.)*

- **◯ Warlock** — *Pact caster · d8 · Charisma.* You get a few **powerful** spell slots that recharge on a
  **short rest**, an at-will magic blast (Eldritch Blast), and customizable Invocations — power lent by an
  otherworldly **Patron**. **Pick if you want:** a signature ranged blast + utility, fewer-but-stronger
  spells. *(Level 1: Pact Magic + **Otherworldly Patron (choose now)** — e.g. the Fiend, the Archfey.)*

- **◯ Wizard** — *Scholar with the biggest toolbox · d6 (fragile) · Intelligence · full spellcaster.* You
  keep a **spellbook** of the largest spell list in the game and **prepare** the right tools each day;
  ritual-cast utility for free. **Pick if you want:** a versatile problem-solver with an answer for
  everything. *(Level 1: spells [a spellbook of six 1st-level spells + 3 cantrips], Arcane Recovery.)*

## STEP 3 — Circle a BACKGROUND  ◯

*Your background is your character's past life. It grants **two skill proficiencies**, some gear, and a
handy **feature** (a non-combat perk).*

- **◯ Acolyte** — *Insight, Religion.* **Shelter of the Faithful:** free care & welcome at temples of your
  faith. *Fits a character who:* served in a temple or holy order.
- **◯ Charlatan** — *Deception, Sleight of Hand.* **False Identity:** a believable second persona; can forge
  papers. *Fits:* a con artist or grifter.
- **◯ Criminal** — *Deception, Stealth.* **Criminal Contact:** a reliable link to the underworld. *Fits:* a
  thief, smuggler, or ex-outlaw.
- **◯ Entertainer** — *Acrobatics, Performance.* **By Popular Demand:** welcome to perform for food &
  lodging. *Fits:* a bard-at-heart, actor, or acrobat.
- **◯ Folk Hero** — *Animal Handling, Survival.* **Rustic Hospitality:** common folk shelter and hide you.
  *Fits:* a humble local who stood up for the little guy.
- **◯ Guild Artisan** — *Insight, Persuasion.* **Guild Membership:** guild backing, lodging, and clout.
  *Fits:* a skilled craftsperson or merchant.
- **◯ Hermit** — *Medicine, Religion.* **Discovery:** you learned a unique, powerful secret in seclusion.
  *Fits:* a recluse, mystic, or seeker.
- **◯ Noble** — *History, Persuasion.* **Position of Privilege:** treated as high-born; easy audiences.
  *Fits:* highborn, wealthy, or well-connected.
- **◯ Outlander** — *Athletics, Survival.* **Wanderer:** flawless geographic memory; find food & water for
  the party. *Fits:* a tribesperson, explorer, or frontier wanderer.
- **◯ Sage** — *Arcana, History.* **Researcher:** if you don't know it, you know where to find it. *Fits:* a
  scholar, librarian, or academic.
- **◯ Sailor** — *Athletics, Perception.* **Ship's Passage:** free passage by sea for you and friends.
  *Fits:* a deckhand, navigator, or (variant) pirate.
- **◯ Soldier** — *Athletics, Intimidation.* **Military Rank:** soldiers recognize your authority; military
  access. *Fits:* a veteran, guard, or mercenary.
- **◯ Urchin** — *Sleight of Hand, Stealth.* **City Secrets:** move through any city at double speed via
  hidden ways. *Fits:* a street kid who grew up rough.

## STEP 4 — Circle an ABILITY-SCORE METHOD  ◯

*Your six abilities — **STR**ength, **DEX**terity, **CON**stitution, **INT**elligence, **WIS**dom,
**CHA**risma — are your raw talent. Put your highest where your **class** wants it (see each class card's
primary ability). We'll add your race's bonuses after.*

- **◯ Standard Array** *(recommended for new players)* — use these six fixed numbers and assign one to each
  ability: **15, 14, 13, 12, 10, 8.** Fast and balanced.
- **◯ Point Buy** — you get **27 points** to custom-build scores from 8 to 15 (before race). More control;
  good if you know what you want. (We'll walk the costs.)
- **◯ Roll** — roll **4d6, drop the lowest die**, six times; assign as you like. Random and swingy — can be
  great or rough.
