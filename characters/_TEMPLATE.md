## 📖 New to D&D? How a turn works *(vets can skip this box)*

- To **do something risky**, you roll a **d20**, add the matching modifier, and compare to a target number
  (the DM sets it, or it's an enemy's AC / your spell DC). Higher is better.
- **Advantage** = roll two d20s, take the higher; **disadvantage** = take the lower.
- **On your turn** you get to **move** (up to your Speed) **and take one action** (Attack, Cast a Spell,
  Dash, Hide, Help, etc.), plus sometimes a quick **bonus action**.
- If you drop to **0 HP** you make **death saving throws** — allies can heal or stabilize you. Don't panic.
