Welcome, hero-to-be

New to D&D? You don't need to know a single rule. Just make choices that sound fun.

The Quest Travelers keep vanishing on the old Briar Road through the Mournwood. The village of Greywater has scraped together a bounty and hired you to find what's taking them — and put a stop to it. The woods feel wrong. You and your companions are the ones brave (or foolish) enough to go in.

How this works

  1. Click through five quick choices: Race, Class, Background, Ability Scores, and a little Personality.
  2. Everything is explained in plain language as you go. Stuck? Pick whatever sounds fun — there are no wrong answers, and every step has a ✨ Surprise me.
  3. At the end, hit Finish and send the result to your DM. They do all the math and hand you a ready-to-play sheet.

You'll start the adventure as a level 3 hero — here you just pick the bones of who you are; your DM levels you the rest of the way. Your progress saves automatically on this device.

A few words you'll see

AC — Armor Class: how hard you are to hit.
HP — Hit Points: your health.
d20 — a 20-sided die; most rolls use it.
Modifier — the +/− you add to a roll.
Race — what you are.
Class — what you do.
