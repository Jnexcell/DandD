Conditions (quick)

  • Blinded — can't see; attacks vs. have adv., its attacks have disadv.
  • Charmed — can't attack charmer; charmer has adv. on social checks.
  • Frightened — disadv. on checks/attacks while source in sight; can't move closer.
  • Grappled — speed 0; ends if grappler incapacitated.
  • Prone — crawl or stand (½ move); melee vs. has adv., ranged disadv.; attacks disadv.
  • Restrained — speed 0; attacks vs. adv., its attacks disadv.; disadv. Dex saves.
  • Poisoned — disadv. on attacks and ability checks.
  • Stunned — incapacitated, can't move, auto-fail Str/Dex saves; attacks vs. adv.
  • Unconscious — incapacitated, prone, drops items; attacks vs. adv., crit if within 5 ft.
  • Incapacitated — no actions or reactions.

Death saves: roll d20 — 10+ success, <10 fail; 3 successes stabilize, 3 fails die; nat 20 = +1 HP, nat 1 = two fails.

Actions in Combat

  • Attack — one attack (more via a feature).
  • Dash — double your move this turn.
  • Disengage — move without provoking opportunity attacks.
  • Dodge — attacks vs. you have disadv.; adv. on Dex saves.
  • Help — give an ally adv. (a check, or their next attack vs. a foe within 5 ft.).
  • Hide — Stealth vs. passive Perception; unseen = adv. to attack.
  • Ready — set a trigger + action; spend your reaction when it fires.
  • Search — Perception/Investigation to find something.
  • Shove — Athletics vs. Athletics/Acrobatics: knock prone or push 5 ft.
  • Grapple — Athletics vs. Athletics/Acrobatics: target grappled (speed 0).

Opportunity attack: a foe leaving your reach on foot provokes one melee attack (your reaction). Improvise freely — say yes, set a DC.

Advantage · Cover · Unseen

  • Advantage — roll 2d20, take higher.
  • Disadvantage — roll 2d20, take lower.
  • They don't stack — one of each cancels to a flat roll.
  • ½ cover — +2 AC & Dex saves.
  • ¾ cover — +5 AC & Dex saves.
  • Total cover — can't be targeted directly.
  • Attacker unseen — advantage.
  • Target unseen — disadvantage.
  • Prone target — melee adv., ranged disadv.
  • Flanking (optional) — adv. if you use it.

Spells in this module

What the cult & the hag actually cast — so you don't break play to look them up.

  • Hold person (Fanatic, 2nd) — humanoid, Wis save DC 11 or paralyzed; re-saves end of each turn. Melee within 5 ft. auto-crits.
  • Command (Fanatic, 1st) — one word, Wis save DC 11 or obeys ("flee", "grovel", "drop").
  • Inflict wounds (Fanatic, 1st) — melee spell atk +3, 3d10 necrotic on hit.
  • Spiritual weapon (Fanatic, 2nd) — bonus-action floating blade, +3 to hit, 1d8+1 force; moves 20 ft./turn.
  • Shield of faith (Fanatic, 1st) — +2 AC to one ally, conc.
  • Sacred flame (Fanatic, cantrip) — Dex save DC 11 or 1d8 radiant; ignores cover.
  • Vicious mockery (Sere, cantrip) — Wis save DC 12 or 1d4 psychic + disadv. on its next attack.
  • Minor illusion / dancing lights (Sere) — sound/image or floating lights to mislead.
