# Monster Index (A–Z)

Alphabetical index of every entry in the Monster Manual → its **printed** MM page. This is the primary "where do I find X?" lookup. Built from the MM Contents (printed pp.4–5); OCR errors in names corrected.

**Source:** Monster Manual Contents (printed pp.4–5). Page-offset reminder: physical PDF page = printed page + 1.

---

## How entries are grouped

Many monsters share one alphabetical heading. If you can't find a creature by its own name, look under its **group**:

- **Angels** (p.15) → Deva, Planetar, Solar
- **Beholders** (p.26) → Beholder, Death Tyrant, Spectator
- **Blights** (p.31) → Needle, Twig, Vine Blight
- **Demons** (p.50) → Balor, Dretch, Glabrezu, Hezrou, Marilith, Nalfeshnee, Quasit, Shadow Demon, Vrock, Yochlol, etc.
- **Devils** (p.66) → Barbed, Bearded, Bone, Chain, Erinyes, Horned, Ice, Imp, Lemure, Pit Fiend, Spined Devil
- **Dinosaurs** (p.79) → Allosaurus, Ankylosaurus, Plesiosaurus, Pteranodon, Triceratops, Tyrannosaurus Rex, Velociraptor
- **Dragons** (p.86) → all chromatic (black, blue, green, red, white) and metallic (brass, bronze, copper, gold, silver) dragons by age (wyrmling → ancient)
- **Elementals** (p.123) → Air, Earth, Fire, Water Elemental
- **Genies** (p.141) → Djinni, Efreeti, (Dao, Marid)
- **Giants** (p.149) → Cloud, Fire, Frost, Hill, Stone, Storm Giant
- **Golems** (p.167) → Clay, Flesh, Iron, Stone Golem
- **Hags** (p.176) → Green, Night, Sea Hag
- **Lycanthropes** (p.206) → Werebear, Wereboar, Wererat, Weretiger, Werewolf
- **Mephits** (p.215) → Dust, Ice, Magma, Mud, Smoke, Steam Mephit
- **Modrons** (p.224) → Monodrone, Duodrone, Tridrone, Quadrone, Pentadrone
- **Mummies** (p.227) → Mummy, Mummy Lord
- **Nagas** (p.233) → Bone, Guardian, Spirit Naga
- **Oozes** (p.240) → Black Pudding, Gelatinous Cube, Gray Ooze, Ochre Jelly
- **Salamanders** (p.265) → Fire Snake, Salamander
- **Skeletons** (p.272) → Skeleton, Minotaur Skeleton, Warhorse Skeleton
- **Sphinxes** (p.280) → Androsphinx, Gynosphinx
- **Vampires** (p.295) → Vampire, Vampire Spawn
- **Yugoloths** (p.311) → Arcanaloth, Mezzoloth, Nycaloth
- **Zombies** (p.315) → Zombie, Ogre Zombie
