# CR 0 Monsters

The weakest creatures in the Monster Manual — each worth **0 or 10 XP**. Many are beasts/critters with no effective attack, and many double as familiars or Druid Wild Shape forms. A handful (Crawling Claw, Homunculus, Lemure, Shrieker) are noncreature oddities listed in the A–Z section.

**Source:** MM stat blocks (printed pages per `monsters-by-cr.md`; PDF offset **+1**, i.e. physical = printed + 1). Mechanics transcribed in our own format; flavor paraphrased to avoid copyright.
