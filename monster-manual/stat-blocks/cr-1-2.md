# CR 1/2 Monsters

Mechanical stat blocks for every CR 1/2 monster in the Monster Manual (each worth 100 XP). Listed alphabetically. Flavor is paraphrased to one line where included; all numbers verified against the PDF.

**Source:** MM stat blocks via `.build/mm.txt` (physical PDF page = printed + 1). Mechanics rendered in this KB's own format; flavor paraphrased, not quoted. Page citations are the **printed** page. (The Tridrone — grouped under CR 1/4 in the roster — is canonically CR 1/2 and is included here. Cross-references Needle Blight → CR 1/4 and Shadow Demon → CR 4 belong to other files.)

### Darkmantle
*Small monstrosity, unaligned* — **CR 1/2 (100 XP)** · MM p.46

- **AC** 11 · **HP** 22 (5d6 + 5) · **Speed** 10 ft., fly 30 ft.
- **STR** 16 (+3) · **DEX** 12 (+1) · **CON** 13 (+1) · **INT** 2 (−4) · **WIS** 10 (+0) · **CHA** 5 (−3)
- **Skills** Stealth +3 · **Senses** blindsight 60 ft., passive Perception 10 · **Languages** —

***Traits:*** ***Echolocation.*** Can't use its blindsight while deafened. ***False Appearance.*** While motionless, indistinguishable from a stalactite or stalagmite.
***Actions:*** ***Crush.*** Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. Hit: 6 (1d6 + 3) bludgeoning damage, and the darkmantle attaches to the target. A creature can detach it with a DC 13 Strength check (action); the darkmantle can detach using 5 ft. of movement. ***Darkness Aura (1/Day).*** A 15-foot radius of magical darkness extends from and moves with it (concentration, up to 10 minutes); darkvision can't penetrate it, and it dispels light from spells of 2nd level or lower that it overlaps.

Hit: 4 (1d6 + 1) piercing damage, or 5 (1d8 + 1) piercing if used with two hands to make a melee attack.
