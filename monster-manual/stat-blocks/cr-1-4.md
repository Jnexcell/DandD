# CR 1/4 Monsters

Mechanical stat blocks for every CR 1/4 monster in the Monster Manual (each worth 50 XP). Listed alphabetically. Flavor is paraphrased to one line where included; all numbers verified against the PDF.

**Source:** MM stat blocks via `.build/mm.txt` (physical PDF page = printed + 1). Mechanics rendered in this KB's own format; flavor paraphrased, not quoted. Page citations are the **printed** page. (The Tridrone, which the roster groups under CR 1/4, is canonically **CR 1/2 (100 XP)** in the MM and is filed in `cr-1-2.md`.)

### Aarakocra
*Medium humanoid (aarakocra), neutral good* — **CR 1/4 (50 XP)** · MM p.12

- **AC** 12 · **HP** 13 (3d8) · **Speed** 20 ft., fly 50 ft.
- **STR** 10 (+0) · **DEX** 14 (+2) · **CON** 10 (+0) · **INT** 11 (+0) · **WIS** 12 (+1) · **CHA** 11 (+0)
- **Skills** Perception +5 · **Senses** passive Perception 15 · **Languages** Auran

***Traits:*** ***Dive Attack.*** If the aarakocra is flying and dives at least 30 ft. straight toward a target and then hits it with a melee weapon attack, the attack deals an extra 3 (1d6) damage.
***Actions:*** ***Talon.*** Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d4 + 2) slashing damage. ***Javelin.*** Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 30/120 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d6 + 2) piercing damage.
*(Five aarakocra can perform a three-turn aerial dance to summon an air elemental.)*
