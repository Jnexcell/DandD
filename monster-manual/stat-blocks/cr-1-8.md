# CR 1/8 Monsters

Mechanical stat blocks for every CR 1/8 monster in the Monster Manual (2014). Each is worth **25 XP**. Entries are alphabetical.

**Source:** MM stat blocks (printed pages cited; PDF offset is +1, i.e. physical = printed + 1). Numbers transcribed and verified against `.build/mm.txt`; mechanics are given in our own format and flavor is paraphrased (no verbatim prose).

### Flumph
*Small aberration, lawful good* — **CR 1/8 (25 XP)** · MM p.135

- **AC** 12 · **HP** 7 (2d6) · **Speed** 5 ft., fly 30 ft.
- **STR** 6 (-2) · **DEX** 15 (+2) · **CON** 10 (+0) · **INT** 14 (+2) · **WIS** 14 (+2) · **CHA** 11 (+0)
- **Skills** Arcana +4, History +4, Religion +4 · **Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 12 · **Languages** understands Undercommon but can't speak, telepathy 60 ft. · **Damage Vulnerabilities** psychic

***Traits:*** ***Advanced Telepathy.*** Perceives the content of any telepathic communication within 60 ft. and can't be surprised by creatures with any form of telepathy. ***Prone Deficiency.*** If knocked prone, roll a die; on an odd result it lands upside-down and is incapacitated, ending the condition with a DC 10 Dexterity save at the end of each of its turns. ***Telepathic Shroud.*** Immune to effects that sense its emotions or read its thoughts, and to all divination spells.
***Actions:*** ***Tendrils.*** Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. Hit: 4 (1d4 + 2) piercing damage plus 2 (1d4) acid damage; at the end of each of its turns the target makes a DC 10 Constitution save, taking 2 (1d4) acid on a failure or ending the recurring acid on a success (lesser restoration also ends it).
***Stench Spray (1/Day).*** Each creature in a 15-ft cone must succeed on a DC 10 Dexterity save or be coated in foul liquid for 1d4 hours; a coated creature is poisoned for the duration, and other creatures within 5 ft. of it are also poisoned (removable with a short rest bathing in water/alcohol/vinegar).

### Stirge
*Tiny beast, unaligned* — **CR 1/8 (25 XP)** · MM p.284

- **STR** 4 (-3) · **DEX** 16 (+3) · **CON** 11 (+0) · **INT** 2 (-4) · **WIS** 8 (-1) · **CHA** 6 (-2)
- **Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 9 · **Languages** —

***Actions:*** ***Blood Drain.*** Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. Hit: 5 (1d4 + 3) piercing damage, and the stirge attaches to the target.
