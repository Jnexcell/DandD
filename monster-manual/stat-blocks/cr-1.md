# CR 1 Monsters

Mechanical stat blocks for every CR 1 monster in the Monster Manual (each worth 200 XP). Listed alphabetically. Flavor is paraphrased to one line where included; all numbers verified against the PDF.

**Source:** MM stat blocks via `.build/mm.txt` (physical PDF page = printed + 1). Mechanics rendered in this KB's own format; flavor paraphrased, not quoted. Page citations are the **printed** page. (Cross-references Pseudodragon and Sprite belong to `cr-1-4.md`; the Pentadrone — rostered at CR 1 — is canonically **CR 2** and lives in `cr-2.md`.) The Faerie Dragon entry below is the red/orange/yellow (CR 1) version; green/blue/indigo/violet are CR 2.

### Animated Armor
*Medium construct, unaligned* — **CR 1 (200 XP)** · MM p.19

- **AC** 18 (natural armor) · **HP** 33 (6d8 + 6) · **Speed** 25 ft.
- **STR** 14 (+2) · **DEX** 11 (+0) · **CON** 13 (+1) · **INT** 1 (−5) · **WIS** 3 (−4) · **CHA** 1 (−5)
- **Damage Immunities** poison, psychic · **Condition Immunities** blinded, charmed, deafened, exhaustion, frightened, paralyzed, petrified, poisoned · **Senses** blindsight 60 ft. (blind beyond this radius), passive Perception 6 · **Languages** —

***Traits:*** ***Antimagic Susceptibility.*** Incapacitated while in an antimagic field; if targeted by *dispel magic*, must succeed on a Constitution save against the caster's spell save DC or fall unconscious for 1 minute. ***False Appearance.*** While motionless, indistinguishable from a normal suit of armor.
***Actions:*** ***Multiattack.*** Makes two melee attacks. ***Slam.*** Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d6 + 2) bludgeoning damage.

### Dryad
*Medium fey, neutral* — **CR 1 (200 XP)** · MM p.121

- **AC** 11 (16 with *barkskin*) · **HP** 22 (5d8) · **Speed** 30 ft.
- **STR** 10 (+0) · **DEX** 12 (+1) · **CON** 11 (+0) · **INT** 14 (+2) · **WIS** 15 (+2) · **CHA** 18 (+4)
