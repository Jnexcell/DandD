# CR 2 Monsters

Mechanical stat blocks for every CR 2 monster in the Monster Manual (each worth 450 XP). Listed alphabetically. Flavor is paraphrased to one line where included; all numbers verified against the PDF.

**Source:** MM stat blocks via `.build/mm.txt` (physical PDF page = printed + 1). Mechanics rendered in this KB's own format; flavor paraphrased, not quoted. Page citations are the **printed** page. (Two corrections vs. the roster: the **Nothic** (rostered CR 3) and the **Pentadrone** (rostered CR 1) are canonically CR 2 and are included here. Conversely, the roster's **Nightmare** at p.235 is canonically **CR 3 (700 XP)** and is filed under CR 3, not here.)

### Allosaurus
*Large beast, unaligned* — **CR 2 (450 XP)** · MM p.79

- **AC** 13 (natural armor) · **HP** 51 (6d10 + 18) · **Speed** 60 ft.
- **STR** 19 (+4) · **DEX** 13 (+1) · **CON** 17 (+3) · **INT** 2 (−4) · **WIS** 12 (+1) · **CHA** 5 (−3)
- **Skills** Perception +5 · **Senses** passive Perception 15 · **Languages** —

***Traits:*** ***Pounce.*** If the allosaurus moves at least 30 ft. straight toward a creature and then hits it with a claw attack on the same turn, that target must succeed on a DC 13 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone. If the target is prone, the allosaurus can make one bite attack against it as a bonus action.
***Actions:*** ***Claw.*** Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d8 + 4) slashing damage. ***Bite.*** Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 15 (2d10 + 4) piercing damage.

### Gelatinous Cube
*Large ooze, unaligned* — **CR 2 (450 XP)** · MM p.242

- **AC** 6 · **HP** 84 (8d10 + 40) · **Speed** 15 ft.
- **STR** 14 (+2) · **DEX** 3 (−4) · **CON** 20 (+5) · **INT** 1 (−5) · **WIS** 6 (−2) · **CHA** 1 (−5)
- **Condition Immunities** blinded, charmed, deafened, exhaustion, frightened, prone · **Senses** blindsight 60 ft. (blind beyond this radius), passive Perception 8 · **Languages** —

***Traits:*** ***Ooze Cube.*** Takes up its entire space. Creatures can enter the space but are subjected to its Engulf and have disadvantage on the save. ***Actions:*** ***Pseudopod.*** Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. Hit: 10 (3d6) acid damage. ***Engulf.*** Moves up to its speed through Large-or-smaller creatures' spaces; each creature whose space it enters makes a DC 12 Dexterity save. On a success the creature may be pushed 5 ft. aside; on a failure it takes 10 (3d6) acid damage, is engulfed (can't breathe, restrained), and takes 21 (6d6) acid damage at the start of each of the cube's turns. An engulfed creature can escape with a DC 12 Strength check.
