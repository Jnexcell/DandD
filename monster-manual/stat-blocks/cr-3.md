# CR 3 Monsters

Mechanical stat blocks for the commonly-used CR 3 monsters in the Monster Manual (each worth 700 XP). Listed alphabetically. Flavor is paraphrased to one line where included; all numbers verified against the PDF. **Note:** CR 3 is a *curated subset* (CR 0–2 are exhaustive; a few CR 3 monsters — e.g. Displacer Beast, Spectator, Water Weird, Killer Whale, Hobgoblin Captain — aren't transcribed yet; look them up in the MM PDF).

**Source:** MM stat blocks via `.build/mm.txt` (physical PDF page = printed + 1). Mechanics rendered in this KB's own format; flavor paraphrased, not quoted. Page citations are the **printed** page. (Two corrections vs. the roster: the **Nothic** (rostered CR 3) is canonically CR 2 and now lives in `cr-2.md`; the **Nightmare** (rostered CR 2) is canonically **CR 3 (700 XP)** and is included here.)

### Basilisk
*Medium monstrosity, unaligned* — **CR 3 (700 XP)** · MM p.24

- **AC** 15 (natural armor) · **HP** 52 (8d8 + 16) · **Speed** 20 ft.
- **STR** 16 (+3) · **DEX** 8 (−1) · **CON** 15 (+2) · **INT** 2 (−4) · **WIS** 8 (−1) · **CHA** 7 (−2)
- **Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 9 · **Languages** —

***Traits:*** ***Petrifying Gaze.*** If a creature starts its turn within 30 ft. of the basilisk and the two can see each other, the basilisk can force it to make a DC 12 Constitution saving throw if the basilisk isn't incapacitated. On a failure, the creature begins to turn to stone and is restrained; it repeats the save at the end of its next turn — success ends the effect, failure petrifies it for 24 hours (a *greater restoration* or similar can end it). A creature can avert its eyes at the start of its turn to avoid the save (disadvantage on attacks against the basilisk that turn); if it looks again, it must immediately save. If the basilisk sees its own reflection in bright light within 30 ft. and has no other creature to look at, it can be caught by its own gaze.
***Actions:*** ***Bite.*** Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 10 (2d6 + 3) piercing damage plus 7 (2d6) poison damage.

### Bearded Devil
*Medium fiend (devil), lawful evil* — **CR 3 (700 XP)** · MM p.70

- **AC** 13 (natural armor) · **HP** 52 (8d8 + 16) · **Speed** 30 ft.
- **STR** 16 (+3) · **DEX** 15 (+2) · **CON** 15 (+2) · **INT** 9 (−1) · **WIS** 11 (+0) · **CHA** 11 (+0)
- **Saving Throws** Str +5, Con +4, Wis +2 · **Damage Resistances** cold; bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical weapons that aren't silvered · **Damage Immunities** fire, poison · **Condition Immunities** poisoned · **Senses** darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 10 · **Languages** Infernal, telepathy 120 ft.

***Traits:*** ***Devil's Sight.*** Magical darkness doesn't impede the devil's darkvision. ***Magic Resistance.*** Advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects. ***Steadfast.*** Can't be frightened while it can see an allied creature within 30 ft. of it.
***Actions:*** ***Multiattack.*** Makes two attacks: one with its beard and one with its glaive. ***Beard.*** Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. Hit: 6 (1d8 + 2) piercing damage, and the target must succeed on a DC 12 Constitution saving throw or be poisoned for 1 minute; while poisoned this way it can't regain hit points (repeat save at end of each of its turns to end). ***Glaive.*** Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d10 + 3) slashing damage. If the target is a creature other than an undead or construct, it must succeed on a DC 12 Constitution saving throw or take 5 (1d10) damage at the start of each of its turns from an infernal wound; each subsequent hit increases that damage by 5 (1d10). A creature can take an action to make a DC 12 Wisdom (Medicine) check to stanch it; magical healing also closes it.

### Doppelganger
*Medium monstrosity (shapechanger), neutral* — **CR 3 (700 XP)** · MM p.82

- **AC** 14 · **HP** 52 (8d8 + 16) · **Speed** 30 ft.
- **STR** 11 (+0) · **DEX** 18 (+4) · **CON** 14 (+2) · **INT** 11 (+0) · **WIS** 12 (+1) · **CHA** 14 (+2)
- **Skills** Deception +6, Insight +3 · **Condition Immunities** charmed · **Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 11 · **Languages** Common

It can keep reading the target's thoughts while in range, as long as its concentration holds (as if concentrating on a spell). ### Green Hag

- **AC** 17 (natural armor) · **HP** 82 (11d8 + 33) · **Speed** 30 ft.
- **STR** 18 (+4) · **DEX** 12 (+1) · **CON** 16 (+3) · **INT** 13 (+1) · **WIS** 14 (+2) · **CHA** 14 (+2)

### Mummy
*Medium undead, lawful evil* — **CR 3 (700 XP)** · MM p.228

- **AC** 11 (natural armor) · **HP** 58 (9d8 + 18) · **Speed** 20 ft.
- **STR** 16 (+3) · **DEX** 8 (−1) · **CON** 15 (+2) · **INT** 6 (−2) · **WIS** 10 (+0) · **CHA** 12 (+1)

A creature that succeeds is immune to the Dreadful Glare of all mummies (but not mummy lords) for 24 hours.
