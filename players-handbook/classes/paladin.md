# Paladin

A holy warrior bound to a **sacred oath** — a durable front-line martial that fuses weapon skill with divine magic. Defined by **Lay on Hands** healing, the burst-damage of **Divine Smite**, the party-wide save bonus of **Aura of Protection**, and an oath chosen at 3rd level that shapes its Channel Divinity and capstone powers. A **half-caster** that prepares spells using Charisma.

**Source:** PHB Chapter 3, "Classes" — Paladin (printed pages 82–88).

---

## Flavor & lore

The paladin is the party's **holy warrior** — a heavily-armored champion who swears a binding **oath** to a cause (justice, the protection of the innocent, vengeance against the wicked) and draws real power from that conviction. Whatever the source of the vow — a god, a chivalric code, a personal reckoning — it is the oath itself, not a deity's say-so, that fuels the magic. Break it badly and the story (not a stat block) decides the fallout.

In play the paladin is a **martial-caster hybrid**: a front-line bruiser who hits hard with weapon and shield, but also wields a focused well of divine magic. The signature move is the **Divine Smite** — burn a spell slot the instant a melee hit lands to dump a burst of radiant damage, no concentration, no casting time, brutal on a crit. Beyond the burst, the paladin is the team's **anchor**: **Lay on Hands** patches up allies, and the standing **auras** (saving-throw bonus, immunity to fear, and more by oath) make everyone standing near them tougher to take down. They lean on Charisma — both the smiting magic and those auras scale with force of personality, the same conviction that powers the oath.

As a **half-caster**, the paladin is no wizard: spellcasting only switches on at character level 2, the slot list stops at 5th level, and spells known are **prepared** from the full paladin list (Cha mod + half level). Think of the magic as a force-multiplier on the martial chassis — buffs, smites, healing, and a few utility picks — rather than the main event. The fantasy is the knight who *means it*: a wall of faith between the darkness and everyone they've sworn to protect.

## Class basics

- **Hit Die:** d10 (per paladin level).
- **Hit Points at 1st level:** 10 + your Constitution modifier.
- **Hit Points at higher levels:** 1d10 (or 6) + Con modifier per level after 1st.
- **Saving throw proficiencies:** Wisdom, Charisma. *(Note: the brief mentioned Str & Cha — the PHB grants **Wisdom & Charisma** saves.)* <!-- VERIFY: PHB p.82 lists Saving Throws: Wisdom, Charisma -->
- **Armor proficiencies:** All armor, shields.
- **Weapon proficiencies:** Simple weapons, martial weapons.
- **Tool proficiencies:** None.
- **Skills:** Choose **two** from Athletics, Insight, Intimidation, Medicine, Persuasion, Religion.
- **Primary ability:** Strength (and Charisma for spells/auras); Constitution secondary.

### Starting equipment (PHB p.82)

Plus whatever your background grants:

- (a) a martial weapon and a shield **or** (b) two martial weapons
- (a) five javelins **or** (b) any simple melee weapon
- (a) a priest's pack **or** (b) an explorer's pack
- Chain mail and a holy symbol

## Class features

### Divine Sense (1st level) (PHB p.84)

As an **action**, until the end of your next turn, you know the location of any **celestial, fiend, or undead** within **60 feet** that isn't behind total cover, and learn its **type** (not its identity). You also detect any place or object **consecrated or desecrated** (as with *hallow*) in that radius. Usable **1 + your Charisma modifier** times; regain all uses on a **long rest**.

### Lay on Hands (1st level) (PHB p.84)

You have a healing **pool** equal to **paladin level × 5** hit points, refreshed on a **long rest**. As an **action**, touch a creature and restore HP up to the amount left in the pool. Alternatively, spend **5 points** from the pool to **cure one disease or neutralize one poison** affecting the target (spend points separately for each condition cured). No effect on **undead or constructs**.

### Fighting Style (2nd level) (PHB p.84)

Choose one (can't pick the same option twice):

- **Defense.** +1 to **AC** while wearing armor.
- **Dueling.** +2 to **damage** with a melee weapon wielded in one hand and no other weapon.
- **Great Weapon Fighting.** Reroll a **1 or 2** on a damage die for a melee weapon wielded with two hands (two-handed or versatile); use the new roll.
- **Protection.** Use your **reaction** to impose **disadvantage** on an attack against a creature within 5 ft of you. Requires a **shield**.

### Spellcasting (2nd level) (PHB p.84)

You cast paladin spells using **Charisma** (drawing on divine power like a cleric). Spells are **prepared**, not known.

- **Spells prepared = Charisma modifier + half your paladin level (rounded down), minimum 1.** Change the list on a long rest (1 minute per spell level of each spell prepared).
- **Spell save DC = 8 + proficiency bonus + Charisma modifier.**
- **Spell attack modifier = proficiency bonus + Charisma modifier.**
- A **holy symbol** works as your spellcasting focus.
- Slots per the table above (half-caster). See `../spells/` and `../10-spellcasting.md` for slot/upcasting rules and the paladin spell list.

### Divine Smite (2nd level) (PHB p.85)

When you **hit with a melee weapon attack**, expend a paladin **spell slot** to deal extra **radiant damage**: **2d8** for a 1st-level slot, **+1d8 per slot level above 1st**, to a **maximum of 5d8** (a 4th-level slot). The damage **increases by 1d8** (so up to 6d8) if the target is an **undead or fiend**. This is not a spell — it doesn't require concentration and isn't affected by counterspell.

### Divine Health (3rd level) (PHB p.85)

You are **immune to disease**.

### Sacred Oath (3rd level) (PHB p.85)

Choose **Oath of Devotion**, **Oath of the Ancients**, or **Oath of Vengeance** (see Subclasses). Grants **Oath Spells**, **Channel Divinity**, and features at **3rd, 7th, 15th, and 20th level**.

- **Oath Spells** are always prepared and **don't count** against your prepared-spells limit. If an oath spell isn't on the paladin list, it counts as a paladin spell for you.
- **Channel Divinity:** choose one of your oath's two options when you use it; then finish a **short or long rest** to use it again. Save DC equals your paladin spell save DC.

### Extra Attack (5th level) (PHB p.85)

Attack **twice** when you take the Attack action.

### Aura of Protection (6th level) (PHB p.85)

While **conscious**, you and friendly creatures within **10 feet** add your **Charisma modifier** (minimum **+1**) to **all saving throws**. The aura's range increases to **30 feet at 18th level**.

### Aura of Courage (10th level) (PHB p.85)

While you are conscious, you and friendly creatures within **10 feet** can't be **frightened**. Range increases to **30 feet at 18th level**.

### Improved Divine Smite (11th level) (PHB p.85)

Every time you **hit with a melee weapon attack**, the target takes an extra **1d8 radiant** damage automatically (no slot spent). If you also use Divine Smite, add this 1d8 to that smite's damage.

### Cleansing Touch (14th level) (PHB p.85)

Use your **action** to **end one spell** on yourself or one willing creature you touch. Usable a number of times equal to your **Charisma modifier** (minimum 1); regain uses on a **long rest**.

### Ability Score Improvement (4th, 8th, 12th, 16th, 19th) (PHB p.84)

Increase one ability by 2, or two by 1 each (max 20). May take a feat instead if your DM uses that rule.

## Subclasses (Sacred Oaths)

Chosen at 3rd level. Each oath provides **Oath Spells**, **two Channel Divinity options** at 3rd, and features at **7th, 15th, and 20th**.

> **Oathbreaker** is a paladin option in the **Dungeon Master's Guide**, not the PHB. The PHB only describes the narrative consequences of **breaking your oath** (DM's discretion); there are no mechanical "fallen paladin" rules in this chapter (PHB p.86).

### Oath of Devotion (PHB p.85–86)

The "knight in shining armor" — bound to justice, virtue, and order (tenets: Honesty, Courage, Compassion, Honor, Duty).

- **Oath Spells:** 3rd *protection from evil and good*, *sanctuary*; 5th *lesser restoration*, *zone of truth*; 9th *beacon of hope*, *dispel magic*; 13th *freedom of movement*, *guardian of faith*; 17th *commune*, *flame strike*.
- **Channel Divinity (3rd):**
  - **Sacred Weapon.** As an action, for **1 minute** add your **Charisma modifier** (min +1) to attack rolls with one weapon you hold; it sheds bright light (20-ft radius, +20 ft dim) and becomes magical. Ends early if you drop/stow it or fall unconscious.
  - **Turn the Unholy.** As an action, each **fiend or undead** within **30 feet** that can see/hear you makes a **Wisdom save** or is **turned** for 1 minute or until it takes damage.
- **Aura of Devotion (7th).** You and friendly creatures within **10 feet** can't be **charmed** while you're conscious (**30 feet** at 18th).
- **Purity of Spirit (15th).** You are permanently under the effects of *protection from evil and good*.
- **Holy Nimbus (20th).** As an action, for **1 minute** emanate sunlight: bright light in a **30-ft radius** (+30 ft dim); enemies that **start their turn** in the bright light take **10 radiant** damage. You also have **advantage on saves** vs. spells cast by fiends/undead. Once per **long rest**.

### Oath of the Ancients (PHB p.86–87)

The "fey/green knight" — devoted to light, life, and beauty over law or chaos (tenets: Kindle the Light, Shelter the Light, Preserve Your Own Light, Be the Light).

- **Oath Spells:** 3rd *ensnaring strike*, *speak with animals*; 5th *moonbeam*, *misty step*; 9th *plant growth*, *protection from energy*; 13th *ice storm*, *stoneskin*; 17th *commune with nature*, *tree stride*.
- **Channel Divinity (3rd):**
  - **Nature's Wrath.** As an action, spectral vines grasp a creature within **10 feet** you can see; it makes a **Strength or Dexterity save** (its choice) or is **restrained**, repeating the save at the end of each of its turns.
  - **Turn the Faithless.** As an action, each **fey or fiend** within **30 feet** that can hear you makes a **Wisdom save** or is **turned** for 1 minute or until it takes damage. Turning also reveals a creature's true form if disguised by illusion/shapeshifting.
- **Aura of Warding (7th).** You and friendly creatures within **10 feet** have **resistance to damage from spells** (**30 feet** at 18th).
- **Undying Sentinel (15th).** When reduced to **0 HP** but not killed outright, you can drop to **1 HP** instead (once per **long rest**). You also no longer age and can't be aged magically.
- **Elder Champion (20th).** As an action, transform for **1 minute**: regain **10 HP** at the start of each of your turns; cast paladin spells with a 1-action casting time as a **bonus action**; enemies within **10 feet** have **disadvantage** on saves vs. your paladin spells and Channel Divinity. Once per **long rest**.

### Oath of Vengeance (PHB p.87–88)

The "avenger / dark knight" — sworn to punish grievous wrongdoers by any means (tenets: Fight the Greater Evil, No Mercy for the Wicked, By Any Means Necessary, Restitution).

- **Oath Spells:** 3rd *bane*, *hunter's mark*; 5th *hold person*, *misty step*; 9th *haste*, *protection from energy*; 13th *banishment*, *dimension door*; 17th *hold monster*, *scrying*.
- **Channel Divinity (3rd):**
  - **Abjure Enemy.** As an action, choose a creature within **60 feet** you can see; unless immune to frightened it makes a **Wisdom save** (fiends/undead at disadvantage). On a failure it's **frightened** for 1 minute (or until it takes damage) and its **speed is 0**; on a success its speed is **halved**.
  - **Vow of Enmity.** As a **bonus action**, gain **advantage on attack rolls** against a creature within **10 feet** for 1 minute or until it drops to 0 HP / falls unconscious.
- **Relentless Avenger (7th).** When you hit with an **opportunity attack**, you can move up to **half your speed** as part of that reaction without provoking opportunity attacks.
- **Soul of Vengeance (15th).** When a creature under your **Vow of Enmity** makes an attack, use your **reaction** to make a **melee weapon attack** against it (if in range).
- **Avenging Angel (20th).** As an action, transform for **1 hour**: gain a **flying speed of 60 feet**; emanate a **30-ft aura of menace** — the first time an enemy enters it or starts its turn there in a battle, it makes a **Wisdom save** or is **frightened** for 1 minute (or until it takes damage), and attacks against that frightened creature have **advantage**. Once per **long rest**.

## Common questions

**Q: Can I use Divine Smite on a critical hit, and do the dice double?**
A: Yes. Divine Smite triggers on a melee weapon **hit**, so it works on a crit, and like all dice from a critical hit, the smite's dice are **doubled** (e.g., a 1st-level smite crit = 4d8 before the undead/fiend bonus, which also doubles). (PHB p.85, p.196)

**Q: Can I decide to Divine Smite *after* I see the attack hits?**
A: Yes. You choose to expend the slot **after the hit is confirmed** but before damage is resolved, which is why it's so reliable. It also doesn't require an action or bonus action. (PHB p.85)

**Q: Does Divine Smite work with ranged or spell attacks?**
A: No — only **melee weapon** attacks (including thrown weapons used in melee at DM discretion, but not a bow). Improved Divine Smite (11th) likewise applies only to melee weapon hits. (PHB p.85)

**Q: How does Aura of Protection stack across a party — does each paladin's aura apply?**
A: A creature in **two paladins' auras** benefits from **both** Charisma bonuses to saves (the feature doesn't say it doesn't stack). The paladin must be **conscious** to project it. At 18th level both Aura of Protection and Aura of Courage extend to **30 feet**. (PHB p.85)

**Q: Are oath spells extra prepared spells?**
A: Yes — oath spells are **always prepared for free** and never count against your "Cha mod + half level" limit. They're paladin spells for you even if not on the normal list. (PHB p.85)
