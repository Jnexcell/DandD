# Cantrips (Level-0 Spells)

Full detail on every level-0 spell (cantrip) in the PHB. Cantrips are always prepared, cost no spell slot, and many damage cantrips scale automatically with **character** level (not slot level) at 5th, 11th, and 17th level.

**Source:** PHB Chapter 11, "Spells" — alphabetical spell descriptions (printed p.211–289). Class associations are from the class spell lists (printed p.207–210). For the rules of casting (components, the spell save DC and attack bonus, concentration), see `../10-spellcasting.md`.

---

## How to read these entries

Each entry gives the spell's school, the classes that have it on their list, the four stat-block lines (Casting Time / Range / Components / Duration), a plain-language effect, and the **cantrip scaling** line for damage cantrips. Components are **V** (verbal), **S** (somatic), **M** (material, with the item in parentheses). Page cites are to the printed PHB.

There are **27 cantrips** in the PHB.

### Acid Splash
*Conjuration cantrip · Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard* (PHB p.211)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Hurl a bubble of acid at one creature in range, or at two creatures in range that are within 5 feet of each other. Each target makes a **Dexterity saving throw** or takes **1d6 acid damage** (no damage on a success).

**Cantrip scaling:** damage increases by 1d6 at 5th level (2d6), 11th (3d6), and 17th (4d6).

### Chill Touch
*Necromancy cantrip · Classes: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard* (PHB p.221)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 120 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 round

A ghostly skeletal hand assails a creature in range. **Ranged spell attack**; on a hit the target takes **1d8 necrotic damage** and can't regain hit points until the start of your next turn. An undead target also has **disadvantage on attack rolls against you** until the end of your next turn.

**Cantrip scaling:** damage increases by 1d8 at 5th level (2d8), 11th (3d8), and 17th (4d8).

### Dancing Lights
*Evocation cantrip · Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Wizard* (PHB p.230)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 120 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a bit of phosphorus or wychwood, or a glowworm)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

Create up to four torch-sized lights (as torches, lanterns, or glowing orbs), or combine them into one vaguely humanoid Medium form. Each light sheds **dim light in a 10-foot radius**. As a bonus action you can move the lights up to 60 feet to a new spot in range; each light must stay within 20 feet of another light from this spell, and a light winks out if it leaves the spell's range.

### Druidcraft
*Transmutation cantrip · Classes: Druid* (PHB p.236)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Whispering to nature spirits, create one of the following within range: a harmless sensory effect predicting the weather for the next 24 hours; instantly make a flower bloom, a seed pod open, or a leaf bud; create an instantaneous, harmless sensory effect (falling leaves, a puff of wind, a small animal's sound, a faint odor); or instantly light or snuff out a candle, torch, or small campfire.

### Eldritch Blast
*Evocation cantrip · Classes: Warlock* (PHB p.237)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 120 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

A beam of crackling energy streaks toward a creature in range. **Ranged spell attack**; on a hit the target takes **1d10 force damage**.

**Cantrip scaling:** the spell creates more beams as you level — **two** beams at 5th level, **three** at 11th, **four** at 17th. You can aim the beams at the same or different targets; make a separate attack roll for each beam. (Note: many Warlock invocations modify this cantrip, e.g. Agonizing Blast adds your Charisma modifier to each beam's damage.)

### Friends
*Enchantment cantrip · Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard* (PHB p.244)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** S, M (a small amount of makeup applied to the face as the spell is cast)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

For the duration you have **advantage on all Charisma checks** directed at one creature of your choice that isn't hostile to you. When the spell ends, the creature realizes you used magic to influence it and **becomes hostile** — it may attack or seek retribution depending on the situation (DM's discretion).

### Light
*Evocation cantrip · Classes: Bard, Cleric, Sorcerer, Wizard* (PHB p.255)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, M (a firefly or phosphorescent moss)
- **Duration:** 1 hour

Touch one object no larger than 10 feet in any dimension. Until the spell ends the object sheds **bright light in a 20-foot radius and dim light for an additional 20 feet**, in a color you choose. Covering the object with something opaque blocks the light. If you target an object held or worn by a hostile creature, that creature makes a **Dexterity saving throw** to avoid the spell. The spell ends if you cast it again or dismiss it as an action.

### Mage Hand
*Conjuration cantrip · Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard* (PHB p.256)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 minute

A spectral floating hand appears at a point in range. It vanishes if it ever ends up more than 30 feet from you or if you cast the spell again. As an action you can control the hand to manipulate an object, open an unlocked door or container, stow or retrieve an item from an open container, or pour out a vial — moving it up to 30 feet each time. The hand **can't attack, activate magic items, or carry more than 10 pounds**.

### Mending
*Transmutation cantrip · Classes: Bard, Cleric, Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard* (PHB p.259)

- **Casting Time:** 1 minute
- **Components:** V, S, M (two lodestones)
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Repair a single break or tear in an object you touch (a broken chain link, two halves of a key, a torn cloak, a leaking wineskin) as long as the break is no larger than 1 foot in any dimension, leaving no trace. It can mend a magic item or construct physically but **can't restore lost magic** to such an object.

### Message
*Transmutation cantrip · Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Wizard* (PHB p.259)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 120 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a short piece of copper wire)
- **Duration:** 1 round

Point at a creature in range and whisper a message; only that target hears it and can whisper a reply only you can hear. You can target through solid objects if you know the target is beyond a barrier, but the spell is blocked by 1 foot of stone, 1 inch of common metal, a thin sheet of lead, or 3 feet of wood. The path can travel around corners and through openings.

### Minor Illusion
*Illusion cantrip · Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard* (PHB p.260)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** S, M (a bit of fleece)
- **Duration:** 1 minute

Create either a **sound** or an **image** of an object within range (not both). A sound can range from a whisper to a scream and can be any sound you choose. An image of an object must be no larger than a **5-foot cube** and creates no sound, light, smell, or other sensory effect. Physical interaction reveals an image is illusory (things pass through it). A creature can use its action to examine the illusion and, with a successful **Intelligence (Investigation) check vs. your spell save DC**, discern it for what it is — after which it becomes faint to that creature.

### Prestidigitation
*Transmutation cantrip · Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard* (PHB p.267)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 10 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Up to 1 hour

A minor magical trick. Create one of the following: an instantaneous harmless sensory effect (sparks, a puff of wind, faint musical notes, an odd odor); instantly light or snuff a candle, torch, or small campfire; instantly clean or soil an object up to 1 cubic foot; chill, warm, or flavor up to 1 cubic foot of nonliving material for 1 hour; make a color, small mark, or symbol appear on an object or surface for 1 hour; or create a nonmagical trinket or illusory image that fits in your hand and lasts until the end of your next turn. You can have up to **three** non-instantaneous effects active at once.

### Produce Flame
*Conjuration cantrip · Classes: Druid* (PHB p.269)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 10 minutes

A flickering flame appears in your hand, harming neither you nor your gear, shedding **bright light in a 10-foot radius and dim light for an additional 10 feet**. You can also hurl the flame at a creature within 30 feet (this ends the spell) — **ranged spell attack**, **1d8 fire damage** on a hit.

**Cantrip scaling:** the thrown attack's damage increases by 1d8 at 5th level (2d8), 11th (3d8), and 17th (4d8).

- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Unleash insults laced with enchantment at a creature you can see in range. If it can hear you (it need not understand you), it makes a **Wisdom saving throw** or takes **1d4 psychic damage** and has **disadvantage on its next attack roll** before the end of its next turn.
