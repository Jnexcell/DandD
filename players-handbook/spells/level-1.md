# 1st-Level Spells

Full detail on every 1st-level spell in the PHB. A 1st-level spell costs a 1st-level (or higher) spell slot. Spells tagged **Ritual** can be cast without a slot if you take 10 extra minutes and the caster has the Ritual Casting feature. The **At Higher Levels** note tells you what an upcast (bigger slot) gains.

**Source:** PHB Chapter 11, "Spells" — alphabetical spell descriptions (printed p.211–289). Class associations are from the class spell lists (printed p.207–210). For casting rules (components, spell save DC and attack bonus, concentration, rituals, upcasting), see `../10-spellcasting.md`.

There are **62 first-level spells** in the PHB.

---

## How to read these entries

Each entry gives school, the classes that have it on their list, the four stat-block lines, a plain-language effect, and (where relevant) an **At Higher Levels** note. Components: **V** verbal, **S** somatic, **M** material (item in parentheses; a gp cost or "which the spell consumes" means you must supply it). Page cites are to the printed PHB.

### Alarm
*Abjuration (ritual) · Classes: Ranger, Wizard* — **Ritual** (PHB p.211)

- **Casting Time:** 1 minute
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a tiny bell and a piece of fine silver wire)
- **Duration:** 8 hours

Set an alarm on a door, window, or area up to a 20-foot cube. Until the spell ends you're alerted whenever a **Tiny or larger creature** touches or enters the warded area (you may designate creatures that won't trip it). A **mental** alarm pings your mind (and wakes you) if you're within 1 mile; an **audible** alarm rings a hand-bell sound for 10 seconds within 60 feet.

### Animal Friendship
*Enchantment · Classes: Bard, Druid, Ranger* (PHB p.212)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a morsel of food)
- **Duration:** 24 hours

Convince a beast you can see (that can see and hear you) that you mean no harm. If its **Intelligence is 4 or higher** the spell fails; otherwise it makes a **Wisdom saving throw** or is **charmed** for the duration. If you or your companions harm it, the spell ends.

**At Higher Levels:** target one additional beast per slot level above 1st.

*Illusion · Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Wizard* (PHB p.233)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 hour

Make yourself — including clothing, armor, weapons, and belongings — look different until the spell ends or you dismiss it. *Abjuration · Classes: Cleric, Paladin* (PHB p.275)

- **Casting Time:** 1 bonus action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a small parchment with a bit of holy text written on it)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes

A shimmering field surrounds a creature of your choice in range, granting it a **+2 bonus to AC** for the duration.

### Silent Image
*Illusion · Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Wizard* (PHB p.276)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a bit of fleece)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes

Create the image of an object, creature, or other visible phenomenon no larger than a **15-foot cube** at a spot in range. The image is **purely visual** (no sound, smell, or other sensory effect). As an action you can move it anywhere in range and alter it to look natural. Physical interaction reveals it as illusory; a creature can use its action to examine it and, with a successful **Intelligence (Investigation) check vs. your spell save DC**, see through it.

### Sleep
*Enchantment · Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Wizard* (PHB p.276)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 90 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a pinch of fine sand, rose petals, or a cricket)
- **Duration:** 1 minute

Roll **5d8** — that total is how many hit points of creatures this spell can affect. Creatures within **20 feet** of a point you choose are affected in ascending order of current HP (ignoring unconscious creatures). Each affected creature falls **unconscious** until the spell ends, it takes damage, or someone uses an action to shake/slap it awake. **Undead and creatures immune to being charmed are unaffected.**

**At Higher Levels:** roll an additional 2d8 per slot level above 1st.

### Speak with Animals
*Divination (ritual) · Classes: Bard, Druid, Ranger* — **Ritual** (PHB p.277)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 10 minutes

For the duration you can **comprehend and verbally communicate with beasts**. Most beasts' knowledge and awareness is limited by their intelligence, but they can at minimum give information about nearby locations and monsters, including what they can perceive or have perceived recently. You might be able to persuade a beast to perform a small favor (DM's discretion).

### Tasha's Hideous Laughter
*Enchantment · Classes: Bard, Wizard* (PHB p.280)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (tiny tarts and a feather waved in the air)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

A creature you can see perceives everything as hilariously funny. It makes a **Wisdom saving throw** or falls **prone, becoming incapacitated and unable to stand** for the duration. A creature with **Intelligence 4 or less** is unaffected. At the end of each of its turns, and each time it takes damage, it can repeat the save (with **advantage if triggered by damage**), ending the spell on a success.

### Tenser's Floating Disk
*Conjuration (ritual) · Classes: Wizard* — **Ritual** (PHB p.282)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a drop of mercury)
- **Duration:** 1 hour

Create a circular horizontal plane of force, **3 feet in diameter and 1 inch thick**, floating 3 feet above the ground in an unoccupied space in range. It holds up to **500 pounds** (excess weight ends the spell). It's immobile while you're within 20 feet; if you move farther, it follows to stay within 20 feet. It can't cross an elevation change of 10 feet or more. If you move more than 100 feet from it, the spell ends.

### Thunderous Smite
*Evocation · Classes: Paladin* (PHB p.282)

- **Casting Time:** 1 bonus action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

The next time you hit with a **melee weapon attack** before the spell ends, the attack deals an extra **2d6 thunder damage** (audible within 300 feet). If the target is a creature, it makes a **Strength saving throw** or is **pushed 10 feet away and knocked prone**.

### Thunderwave
*Evocation · Classes: Bard, Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard* (PHB p.282)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self (15-foot cube)
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

A wave of thunderous force sweeps out from you. Each creature in a **15-foot cube** makes a **Constitution saving throw**: on a failure, **2d8 thunder damage** and it is **pushed 10 feet** away from you; on a success, half damage and no push. Unsecured objects in the area are also pushed 10 feet, and the spell emits a boom audible out to 300 feet.

**At Higher Levels:** damage increases by 1d8 per slot level above 1st.

### Unseen Servant
*Conjuration (ritual) · Classes: Bard, Warlock, Wizard* — **Ritual** (PHB p.284)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a piece of string and a bit of wood)
- **Duration:** 1 hour

Create an invisible, mindless, shapeless force that performs simple tasks. It has **AC 10, 1 HP, Strength 2**, and can't attack; if it drops to 0 HP the spell ends. Once per turn as a **bonus action** you can command it to move up to 15 feet and interact with an object — fetching, cleaning, mending, folding clothes, lighting fires, serving food, pouring wine, etc. If a task would move it more than 60 feet from you, the spell ends.

### Witch Bolt
*Evocation · Classes: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard* (PHB p.289)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a twig from a tree struck by lightning)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

A beam of crackling blue energy lances toward a creature in range, forming a sustained arc. **Ranged spell attack**; on a hit the target takes **1d12 lightning damage**, and on each of your later turns you can use your **action to deal 1d12 lightning damage automatically**. The spell ends if you use your action for anything else, or if the target ever leaves the spell's range or gains total cover from you.

**At Higher Levels:** the **initial** damage increases by 1d12 per slot level above 1st (the automatic ongoing damage stays 1d12).

### Wrathful Smite
*Evocation · Classes: Paladin* (PHB p.289)

- **Casting Time:** 1 bonus action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

The next time you hit with a **melee weapon attack** before the spell ends, the attack deals an extra **1d6 psychic damage**, and if the target is a creature it makes a **Wisdom saving throw** or is **frightened of you** until the spell ends. As an action the frightened creature can make a **Wisdom check vs. your spell save DC** to end the effect.
