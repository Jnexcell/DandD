# 2nd-Level Spells

Full detail on every 2nd-level spell in the PHB. A 2nd-level spell costs a 2nd-level (or higher) spell slot. Spells tagged **Ritual** can be cast without a slot if you take 10 extra minutes and the caster has the Ritual Casting feature. The **At Higher Levels** note tells you what an upcast (bigger slot) gains.

**Source:** PHB Chapter 11, "Spells" — alphabetical spell descriptions (printed p.211–289). Class associations are from the class spell lists (printed p.207–210). For casting rules (components, spell save DC and attack bonus, concentration, rituals, upcasting), see `../10-spellcasting.md`.

There are **59 second-level spells** in the PHB.

---

## How to read these entries

Each entry gives school, the classes that have it on their list, the four stat-block lines, a plain-language effect, and (where relevant) an **At Higher Levels** note. Components: **V** verbal, **S** somatic, **M** material (item in parentheses; a gp cost or "which the spell consumes" means you must supply it). Page cites are to the printed PHB.

### Aid
*Abjuration · Classes: Cleric, Paladin* (PHB p.211)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a tiny strip of white cloth)
- **Duration:** 8 hours

Bolster up to **three creatures** of your choice in range. Each target's **hit point maximum and current hit points increase by 5** for the duration.

**At Higher Levels:** the hit point increase rises by an additional 5 per slot level above 2nd.

### Alter Self
*Transmutation · Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard* (PHB p.211)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour

Assume a different form. Choose one of three options (and you can swap to another as an action while the spell lasts):
- **Aquatic Adaptation** — you can breathe underwater and gain a swimming speed equal to your walking speed.
- **Change Appearance** — alter your looks (height, weight, features, voice, etc.); you can appear as another race, but your statistics don't change, your size stays the same, and your basic body shape is unchanged.
- **Natural Weapons** — grow claws, fangs, horns, or similar; your **unarmed strikes deal 1d6** bludgeoning/piercing/slashing, you're proficient with them, and the natural weapon is magical with a **+1 bonus** to attack and damage.

### Animal Messenger
*Enchantment (ritual) · Classes: Bard, Druid, Ranger* — **Ritual** (PHB p.212)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a morsel of food)
- **Duration:** 24 hours

Use a **Tiny beast** you can see (squirrel, blue jay, bat, etc.) to carry a message of up to **25 words** to a recipient you describe at a location you've visited. The beast travels for the duration (about **50 miles per 24 hours** if it flies, **25 miles** otherwise) and recites the message in your voice to a matching creature. If it doesn't arrive before the spell ends, the message is lost.

**At Higher Levels:** the duration increases by 48 hours per slot level above 2nd.

### Arcane Lock
*Abjuration · Classes: Wizard* (PHB p.215)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (gold dust worth at least 25 gp, which the spell consumes)
- **Duration:** Until dispelled

Lock a door, window, gate, chest, or other entryway. You and creatures you designate can open it normally, and you can set a password that suppresses the lock for 1 minute. Otherwise it's impassable, and the **DC to break or pick it increases by 10**. Casting *knock* on it suppresses the spell for 10 minutes.

### Augury
*Divination (ritual) · Classes: Cleric* — **Ritual** (PHB p.215)

- **Casting Time:** 1 minute
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S, M (specially marked sticks, bones, or tokens worth at least 25 gp)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Receive an omen about a specific course of action you plan to take **within the next 30 minutes**. The DM chooses one of: **Weal** (good), **Woe** (bad), **Weal and Woe** (both), or **Nothing** (neither especially good nor bad). The spell doesn't account for changing circumstances. Casting it more than once before your next long rest raises the chance of a random reading.

### Darkness
*Evocation · Classes: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard* (PHB p.230)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, M (bat fur and a drop of pitch or piece of coal)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes

Magical darkness fills a **15-foot-radius sphere**, spreading around corners. **Darkvision can't see through it** and nonmagical light can't illuminate it. If the area overlaps light created by a spell of **2nd level or lower**, that light spell is dispelled.

### Detect Thoughts
*Divination · Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Wizard* (PHB p.231)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S, M (a copper piece)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

Read the thoughts of certain creatures. When you cast it and as an action each turn, focus on one creature within 30 feet to learn its **surface thoughts** (a creature with **Intelligence 3 or lower** or that speaks no language is unaffected). As an action you can **probe deeper**: the target makes a **Wisdom saving throw** — on a failure you learn its reasoning, emotions, and a prominent concern; on a success the spell ends. Either way it knows you're probing, and can use its action to make an **Intelligence check contested by your Intelligence** to end the spell. You can also search for thinking creatures within 30 feet you can't see (2 ft of rock, 2 in of metal other than lead, or a thin sheet of lead blocks this).

### Find Steed
*Conjuration · Classes: Paladin* (PHB p.240)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Summon a spirit that takes the form of a loyal **steed** (warhorse, pony, camel, elk, mastiff, or another Large or smaller mount at the DM's option). It's a **celestial, fey, or fiend** with the chosen form's statistics; if its Intelligence is 5 or less it becomes 6, and it understands a language you speak.
