# 3rd-Level Spells

Full detail on every 3rd-level spell in the PHB. A 3rd-level spell costs a 3rd-level (or higher) spell slot. Spells tagged **Ritual** can be cast without a slot if you take 10 extra minutes and the caster has the Ritual Casting feature. The **At Higher Levels** note tells you what an upcast (bigger slot) gains.

**Source:** PHB Chapter 11, "Spells" — alphabetical spell descriptions (printed p.211–289). Class associations are from the class spell lists (printed p.207–210). For casting rules (components, spell save DC and attack bonus, concentration, rituals, upcasting), see `../10-spellcasting.md`.

There are **50 third-level spells** in the PHB.

---

## How to read these entries

Each entry gives school, the classes that have it on their list, the four stat-block lines, a plain-language effect, and (where relevant) an **At Higher Levels** note. Components: **V** verbal, **S** somatic, **M** material (item in parentheses; a gp cost or "which the spell consumes" means you must supply it). Page cites are to the printed PHB.

### Animate Dead
*Necromancy · Classes: Cleric, Wizard* (PHB p.212)

- **Casting Time:** 1 minute
- **Range:** 10 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a drop of blood, a piece of flesh, and a pinch of bone dust)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Target a pile of bones or a corpse of a Medium or Small humanoid in range and raise it as an undead servant — a **skeleton** (from bones) or a **zombie** (from a corpse). On your turn you can mentally command undead you've animated (bonus action to issue commands to any number within 60 feet). To keep control, you must recast this spell on the creatures **before the current 24-hour control window ends** — each recast reasserts control over **up to four** of your undead and extends control 24 hours. After 24 hours without reasserting, you lose control (the creature becomes hostile).

**At Higher Levels:** you animate or reassert control over **two additional** undead per slot level above 3rd. Each must come from a different corpse/pile of bones.

### Bestow Curse
*Necromancy · Classes: Bard, Cleric, Wizard* (PHB p.218)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

Touch a creature; it makes a **Wisdom saving throw** or is cursed. Choose one effect:
- **Disadvantage** on ability checks and saving throws made with one ability score of your choice.
- **Disadvantage** on attack rolls against you.
- Each of its turns, it must succeed on a **Wisdom save** or waste its action doing nothing.
- Your attacks and spells deal an extra **1d8 necrotic damage** to the target.
(The DM may allow other curses.)

**At Higher Levels:** **4th** slot = concentration up to 10 minutes; **5th** = 8 hours; **7th** = 24 hours; **9th** = until dispelled (and these durations no longer require concentration).

### Blink
*Transmutation · Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard* (PHB p.219)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 minute

At the **end of each of your turns**, roll a **d20**. On an **11 or higher**, you vanish into the Ethereal Plane until the start of your next turn, then reappear in an unoccupied space of your choice within **10 feet** of where you left. While ethereal you can't be perceived, can't affect or be affected by anything on your plane, and can see/hear about 60 feet into your own plane (everything appears gray and indistinct). (No concentration.)

### Call Lightning
*Conjuration · Classes: Druid* (PHB p.220)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 120 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes

A storm cloud — a **60-foot-radius, 10-foot-high cylinder** — forms centered on a point you can see about **100 feet directly above you**. When you cast it and as an **action** on each later turn, you call a lightning bolt down on a point you can see within range: each creature within **5 feet** of that point makes a **Dexterity saving throw**, taking **3d10 lightning damage** on a failure or half on a success. If you're outdoors in stormy conditions, you instead seize control of the existing storm and the damage is **+1d10**.

**At Higher Levels:** damage increases by 1d10 per slot level above 3rd.

### Counterspell
*Abjuration · Classes: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard* (PHB p.228)

- **Casting Time:** 1 reaction, which you take when you see a creature within 60 feet casting a spell
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Interrupt a creature in the process of casting a spell. If the spell being cast is **3rd level or lower, it automatically fails**. If it's **4th level or higher**, make an ability check using your spellcasting ability — **DC 10 + the spell's level**; on a success, the spell fails and has no effect.

**At Higher Levels:** if you cast it with a slot of the interrupted spell's level **or higher**, it counters automatically (no check).

### Stinking Cloud
*Conjuration · Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Wizard* (PHB p.278)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 90 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a rotten egg or several skunk cabbage leaves)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

A **20-foot-radius sphere** of yellow, nauseating gas appears, **heavily obscuring** its area. A creature that **starts its turn** in the cloud must make a **Constitution saving throw** or **lose its action that turn** (it spends the turn retching and reeling; it can still move). A strong wind disperses the cloud, ending the spell.

### Tongues
*Divination · Classes: Bard, Cleric, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard* (PHB p.284)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, M (a small clay model of a ziggurat)
- **Duration:** 1 hour

The creature you touch gains the ability to **understand any spoken language** it hears. Moreover, **any creature that knows at least one language** and can hear the target **understands what the target says**.

### Vampiric Touch
*Necromancy · Classes: Warlock, Wizard* (PHB p.285)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

Make a **melee spell attack** against a creature within your reach. On a hit, the target takes **3d6 necrotic damage**, and **you regain hit points equal to half** the damage dealt. Until the spell ends, you can make the attack again on each of your turns as an **action**.

**At Higher Levels:** damage increases by 1d6 per slot level above 3rd.

### Water Breathing
*Transmutation (ritual) · Classes: Druid, Ranger, Sorcerer, Wizard* — **Ritual** (PHB p.287)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a short reed or piece of straw)
- **Duration:** 24 hours

Up to **ten willing creatures** you can see in range gain the ability to **breathe underwater** until the spell ends (they retain their normal mode of respiration as well).

### Water Walk
*Transmutation (ritual) · Classes: Cleric, Druid, Ranger, Sorcerer* — **Ritual** (PHB p.287)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a piece of cork)
- **Duration:** 1 hour

Up to **ten willing creatures** gain the ability to **move across any liquid surface** — water, acid, mud, snow, quicksand, lava — as if it were solid ground (lava's heat still harms a creature above it). A submerged creature can be targeted; it rises toward the surface at **60 feet per round**.

### Wind Wall
*Evocation · Classes: Druid, Ranger* (PHB p.288)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 120 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a tiny fan and a feather of exotic origin)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

A wall of strong wind rises — up to **50 feet long, 15 feet high, and 1 foot thick** (shaped as you like) for the duration. A creature in the wall's area when it appears makes a **Strength saving throw**, taking **3d8 bludgeoning damage** on a failure or half on a success. The wall **deflects small flying creatures, loose objects, gases, and most ranged weapon attacks** (arrows, bolts, sling stones) that pass through it.
