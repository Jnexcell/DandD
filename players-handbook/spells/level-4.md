# 4th-Level Spells

Full detail on every 4th-level spell in the PHB. A 4th-level spell costs a 4th-level (or higher) spell slot. Spells tagged **Ritual** can be cast without a slot if you take 10 extra minutes and the caster has the Ritual Casting feature. The **At Higher Levels** note tells you what an upcast (bigger slot) gains.

**Source:** PHB Chapter 11, "Spells" — alphabetical spell descriptions (printed p.211–289). Class associations are from the class spell lists (printed p.207–210). For casting rules (components, spell save DC and attack bonus, concentration, rituals, upcasting), see `../10-spellcasting.md`.

There are **35 fourth-level spells** in the PHB. Wizard-named spells (Evard's, Leomund's, Mordenkainen's, Otiluke's) are alphabetized here under the wizard's name, matching the PHB.

---

## How to read these entries

Each entry gives school, the classes that have it on their list, the four stat-block lines, a plain-language effect, and (where relevant) an **At Higher Levels** note. Components: **V** verbal, **S** somatic, **M** material (item in parentheses; a gp cost or "which the spell consumes" means you must supply it). Page cites are to the printed PHB.

### Arcane Eye
*Divination · Classes: Wizard* (PHB p.214)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a bit of bat fur)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour

Create an invisible, magical eye in range that floats in the air and sends you visual information. The eye has **darkvision out to 30 feet** and sees in all directions. As an **action**, you can move it up to **30 feet** in any direction (including vertically). It can pass through openings as small as **1 inch** in diameter but can't pass through solid matter.

### Aura of Life
*Abjuration · Classes: Paladin* (PHB p.216)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self (30-foot radius)
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes

A life-preserving aura radiates **30 feet** from you. Friendly creatures in the aura (**including you**) have **resistance to necrotic damage**, and their **hit point maximum can't be reduced**. In addition, a friendly creature with **0 hit points** that starts its turn in the aura **regains 1 hit point**.

### Aura of Purity
*Abjuration · Classes: Paladin* (PHB p.216)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self (30-foot radius)
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes

A purifying aura radiates **30 feet** from you. Friendly creatures in the aura (**including you**) **can't become diseased**, have **resistance to poison damage**, and have **advantage on saving throws against effects that cause** the blinded, charmed, deafened, frightened, paralyzed, poisoned, or stunned conditions.

### Banishment
*Abjuration · Classes: Cleric, Paladin, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard* (PHB p.217)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (an item distasteful to the target)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

A creature you can see makes a **Charisma saving throw** or is banished. If the target is **native to your current plane**, it's sent to a harmless demiplane (incapacitated there) and **returns** to the space it left when the spell ends. If the target is **native to a different plane**, it's sent home; if the spell lasts the full **1 minute**, the target **stays there permanently** (doesn't return).

**At Higher Levels:** target one additional creature per slot level above 4th.

### Blight
*Necromancy · Classes: Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard* (PHB p.219)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Necromantic energy drains moisture and vitality from one creature you can see. It makes a **Constitution saving throw**, taking **8d8 necrotic damage** on a failure or half on a success. **No effect on undead or constructs.** A **plant creature or magical plant** has **disadvantage** on the save and takes **maximum damage**; a nonmagical plant (like a tree) that isn't a creature simply withers and dies (no save).

**At Higher Levels:** damage increases by 1d8 per slot level above 4th.

### Compulsion
*Enchantment · Classes: Bard* (PHB p.224)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

Creatures of your choice within 30 feet make a **Wisdom saving throw** or become affected. On each of your turns until the spell ends, you can use a **bonus action** to designate a direction (horizontal to you); each affected creature must spend its turn **moving in that direction** using as much of its movement as possible (it can take its action first, and it can stop to avoid obvious hazards like a cliff or fire). An affected creature can repeat the **Wisdom save** at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.

### Confusion
*Enchantment · Classes: Bard, Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard* (PHB p.224)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 90 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (three nut shells)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

Each creature in a **10-foot-radius sphere** centered on a point in range makes a **Wisdom saving throw** or is affected. At the **start of each of its turns**, an affected creature rolls a **d10** to determine its behavior:
- **1** — moves a random direction (roll a d8) using all its speed, takes no action.
- **2–6** — doesn't move or take actions.
- **7–8** — uses its action to make a melee attack against a randomly determined creature in reach (or nothing, if none).
- **9–10** — acts normally.
A creature repeats the **Wisdom save at the end of each of its turns**, ending the effect on itself on a success.

**At Higher Levels:** the sphere's radius increases by 5 feet per slot level above 4th.

### Conjure Minor Elementals
*Conjuration · Classes: Druid, Wizard* (PHB p.226)

- **Casting Time:** 1 minute
- **Range:** 90 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour

Summon elementals: **one CR 2, two CR 1, four CR 1/2, or eight CR 1/4** (your choice; the DM has their stats). They're friendly, obey your verbal commands (no action), and act on your initiative; they vanish at 0 HP or when the spell ends.

**At Higher Levels:** with a **6th-level slot** choose twice as many, with an **8th** three times as many.

### Control Water
*Transmutation · Classes: Cleric, Druid, Wizard* (PHB p.227)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 300 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a drop of water and a pinch of dust)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes

Control water within a **100-foot cube** in range. Choose one effect (you can change it as an action on later turns):
- **Flood** — raise the water level by up to 20 feet (or, in an ocean, form a 20-foot-tall wave that sweeps creatures).
- **Part Water** — open a trench through a body of water, with walls to either side.
- **Redirect Flow** — make flowing water move in a direction you choose.
- **Whirlpool** — form a vortex; a creature within 25 feet makes a **Strength saving throw**, on a failure is pulled toward the center and takes **2d8 bludgeoning damage**.

### Death Ward
*Abjuration · Classes: Cleric, Paladin* (PHB p.230)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 8 hours

Place a ward on a creature you touch. The **first time** the target would drop to **0 hit points** from taking damage, it instead drops to **1 hit point** (and the spell ends). If an effect would **kill it instantly** without dealing damage, that effect is **negated** against it (and the spell ends). The ward triggers only once.

### Dimension Door
*Conjuration · Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard* (PHB p.233)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 500 feet
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Teleport yourself up to **500 feet** to a spot you can see, visualize, or describe by direction and distance. You can bring along **one willing creature** of your size or smaller that's within 5 feet of you. If you'd arrive in a space occupied by a creature or solid object, you and any companion each take **4d6 force damage** and the teleport fails.

### Divination
*Divination (ritual) · Classes: Cleric* — **Ritual** (PHB p.234)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S, M (incense and a sacrificial offering appropriate to your religion, together worth at least 25 gp, which the spell consumes)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Contact a god or divine servant and receive a **truthful reply** about a specific goal, event, or activity to occur **within 7 days** — usually a short phrase, cryptic rhyme, or omen. The spell doesn't account for changing circumstances. If you cast it **two or more times before a long rest**, there's a **cumulative 25% chance** for each casting after the first to get a random reading.

### Dominate Beast
*Enchantment · Classes: Druid, Sorcerer* (PHB p.235)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

A beast you can see makes a **Wisdom saving throw** or becomes **charmed** for the duration. You have a **telepathic link** with it while on the same plane and can issue commands (no action), which it does its best to obey. As an **action** you can take **total control** until the end of your next turn (precise movement/action). Each time the target **takes damage**, it repeats the **Wisdom save**, ending the spell on a success.

**At Higher Levels:** **5th** slot = concentration up to 10 minutes; **6th** = up to 1 hour; **7th+** = up to 8 hours.

### Evard's Black Tentacles
*Conjuration · Classes: Wizard* (PHB p.238)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 90 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a piece of tentacle from a giant octopus or giant squid)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

Writhing black tentacles fill a **20-foot square** on the ground, making it **difficult terrain**. A creature in the area when you cast it, or that **enters** the area on its turn, must make a **Dexterity saving throw** or take **3d6 bludgeoning damage** and be **restrained**. A creature that **starts its turn** there and is already restrained takes **3d6 bludgeoning damage**. A restrained creature can use its action to make a **Strength or Dexterity check vs. your spell save DC** to free itself.

### Fabricate
*Transmutation · Classes: Wizard* (PHB p.239)

- **Casting Time:** 10 minutes
- **Range:** 120 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Convert raw materials into products of the **same material**: e.g., timber into a bridge, cloth into clothes. You can fabricate a Large or smaller object (up to a **10-foot cube**, or eight connected 5-foot cubes) for nonmineral material, or a Medium or smaller object for mineral material (stone, metal). The quality matches your **tool proficiency** for the craft. You **can't create or transmute creatures or magic items**, and the form must be feasible.

### Fire Shield
*Evocation · Classes: Wizard* (PHB p.242)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S, M (a bit of phosphorus or a firefly)
- **Duration:** 10 minutes

Wreathe yourself in flickering flame that sheds bright light in a 10-foot radius (and dim for another 10). Choose one:
- **Warm shield** — grants you **resistance to cold damage**.
- **Chill shield** — grants you **resistance to fire damage**.
Whenever a creature **within 5 feet** hits you with a **melee attack**, the shield erupts: the attacker takes **2d8 fire damage** (warm) or **2d8 cold damage** (chill).

### Freedom of Movement
*Abjuration · Classes: Bard, Cleric, Druid, Ranger* (PHB p.244)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (a leather strap, bound around the arm or a similar appendage)
- **Duration:** 1 hour

A willing creature you touch is unhindered for the duration: **difficult terrain costs it no extra movement**; spells and effects **can't reduce its speed** or cause it to be **paralyzed or restrained**; it can spend **5 feet of movement** to escape from nonmagical restraints or a grapple; and **being underwater** imposes no penalties on its movement or attacks.

### Guardian of Faith
*Conjuration · Classes: Cleric* (PHB p.247)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** 8 hours

A Large spectral guardian appears and hovers in an unoccupied space you choose. ### Hallucinatory Terrain
*Illusion · Classes: Bard, Druid, Warlock, Wizard* (PHB p.249)

- **Casting Time:** 10 minutes
- **Range:** 300 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a stone, a twig, and a bit of green plant)
- **Duration:** 24 hours

Make **natural terrain** in a **150-foot cube** look, sound, and smell like a different kind of natural terrain (open fields can seem a forest, etc.). ### Ice Storm
*Evocation · Classes: Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard* (PHB p.252)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 300 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a pinch of dust and a few drops of water)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

A hail of rock-hard ice pounds a **20-foot-radius, 40-foot-high cylinder** centered on a point in range. Each creature in the area makes a **Dexterity saving throw**, taking **2d8 bludgeoning damage plus 4d6 cold damage** on a failure or half on a success. The hail turns the ground in the area into **difficult terrain** until the end of your next turn.

**At Higher Levels:** the **bludgeoning** damage increases by 1d8 per slot level above 4th.

### Leomund's Secret Chest
*Conjuration · Classes: Wizard* (PHB p.255)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (an exquisite chest, 3 ft × 2 ft × 2 ft, worth at least 5,000 gp, and a Tiny replica of the same materials worth at least 50 gp)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Hide the chest (and up to **12 cubic feet** of nonliving contents) on the **Ethereal Plane**. With the replica in hand, you can use an action to **recall the chest** (it appears in an unoccupied space within 1 mile) or **send it back** to the Ethereal Plane. After **60 days**, there's a cumulative chance each day the spell ends and the chest is lost; losing or destroying the replica also strands the chest. *(Item costs make this a non-combat utility/storage spell.)*

### Locate Creature
*Divination · Classes: Bard, Cleric, Druid, Paladin, Ranger, Wizard* (PHB p.256)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S, M (a bit of fur from a bloodhound)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour

Sense the **direction to a creature** if it's within **1,000 feet** — either a specific creature you're familiar with, or the nearest creature of a specific kind (e.g., the nearest unicorn). If the creature has changed form (e.g., by *polymorph*), the spell doesn't locate it. **Running water at least 10 feet wide** between you and the target **blocks** the spell.

### Mordenkainen's Faithful Hound
*Conjuration · Classes: Wizard* (PHB p.257)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a tiny silver whistle, a piece of bone, and a thread)
- **Duration:** 8 hours

Conjure an **invisible** phantom watchdog at a point in range, where it remains for the duration. It **can't be harmed**, and it **barks loudly** if any Small or larger creature comes within **30 feet** without first speaking your password (defeating surprise); it also sees invisible creatures, sees into the Ethereal Plane, and ignores illusions. At the **start of each of your turns**, the hound bites one creature **hostile to you within 5 feet** of it — attack bonus equal to **your spellcasting ability modifier + proficiency bonus** — dealing **4d8 piercing damage** on a hit. The spell ends if you move more than **100 feet** from it. (No concentration.)

### Mordenkainen's Private Sanctum
*Abjuration · Classes: Wizard* (PHB p.257)

- **Casting Time:** 10 minutes
- **Range:** 120 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a thin sheet of lead, a piece of opaque glass, a wad of cotton or cloth, and powdered chrysolite)
- **Duration:** 24 hours

Secure an area against scrying and intrusion. The warded area can be a cube from **5 feet up to 100 feet** on a side. Choose any of these properties: **sound can't pass** the boundary; **vision can't pass** (darkness/fog from outside); **divination magic** can't target or sense within; **planar travel and teleportation** to/from the area are blocked. Casting it on the **same spot every day for a year** makes the effect permanent.

**At Higher Levels:** you can increase the maximum cube dimension by **100 feet** per slot level above 4th.

### Otiluke's Resilient Sphere
*Evocation · Classes: Wizard* (PHB p.262)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a hemispherical piece of clear crystal and a matching hemisphere of gum arabic)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

A sphere of shimmering force encloses a creature or object (**Large or smaller**) in range. An unwilling creature makes a **Dexterity saving throw**; on a success it's unaffected. **Nothing — not physical objects, energy, or other spell effects — can pass through the sphere's wall** (in or out), so the enclosed creature is sealed off (it can breathe). The sphere is **weightless**; the creature inside can use its movement to **roll it at half speed** (and can't otherwise leave). It can be picked up. The wall can be destroyed only by *disintegrate* (which makes it vanish).

### Phantasmal Killer
*Illusion · Classes: Wizard* (PHB p.265)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 120 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

Tap into a creature's nightmares to create an illusory manifestation of its **deepest fears**, visible only to it. The target makes a **Wisdom saving throw** or becomes **frightened** for the duration. At the **start of each of its turns**, it must repeat the **Wisdom save**, taking **4d10 psychic damage** on a failure; on a success, the spell ends.

**At Higher Levels:** damage increases by 1d10 per slot level above 4th.

### Polymorph
*Transmutation · Classes: Bard, Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard* (PHB p.266)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a caterpillar cocoon)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour

Transform a creature you can see into a **new form — a beast** whose challenge rating is **no higher than the target's CR (or level)**. An unwilling target makes a **Wisdom saving throw** to avoid the effect. The new form replaces the target's game statistics (it keeps its **alignment and personality** and its **Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma** scores, but can't speak or cast spells). It gains the beast's **hit points**; when those drop to 0 it **reverts** to its true form (excess damage carries over), and it reverts if it dies, falls unconscious, or the spell ends.

### Wall of Fire
*Evocation · Classes: Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard* (PHB p.285)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 120 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a small piece of phosphorus)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

Create a wall of fire on a solid surface — a straight wall up to **60 feet long, 20 feet high, and 1 foot thick**, or a ringed wall up to **20 feet in diameter, 20 feet high, 1 foot thick**. The wall is opaque. When it appears, each creature in its area makes a **Dexterity saving throw**, taking **5d8 fire damage** on a failure or half on a success. **One side** of the wall (your choice) deals damage to a creature that **ends its turn within 10 feet** of that side or **inside** the wall; the other side deals no damage.

**At Higher Levels:** damage increases by 1d8 per slot level above 4th.
