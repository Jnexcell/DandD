# 5th-Level Spells

Full detail on every 5th-level spell in the PHB. A 5th-level spell costs a 5th-level (or higher) spell slot. Spells tagged **Ritual** can be cast without a slot if you take 10 extra minutes and the caster has the Ritual Casting feature. The **At Higher Levels** note tells you what an upcast (bigger slot) gains.

**Source:** PHB Chapter 11, "Spells" — alphabetical spell descriptions (printed p.211–289). Class associations are from the class spell lists (printed p.207–210). For casting rules (components, spell save DC and attack bonus, concentration, rituals, upcasting), see `../10-spellcasting.md`.

There are **42 fifth-level spells** in the PHB. Wizard-named spells (Bigby's, Rary's) are alphabetized here under the wizard's name, matching the PHB.

---

## How to read these entries

Each entry gives school, the classes that have it on their list, the four stat-block lines, a plain-language effect, and (where relevant) an **At Higher Levels** note. Components: **V** verbal, **S** somatic, **M** material (item in parentheses; a gp cost or "which the spell consumes" means you must supply it). Page cites are to the printed PHB.

### Animate Objects
*Transmutation · Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Wizard* (PHB p.213)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 120 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

Bring up to **ten nonmagical objects** (not worn or carried) to life as constructs under your control. Size determines how many of your "ten" each one costs: **Tiny/Small/Medium = 1 each, Large = 2, Huge = 4**. You command them as a **bonus action** (one general command to all, or redirect them); they act on your initiative. Animated-object stats by size (the DM has the full block):

| Size | AC | HP | Attack (to hit) | Damage |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Tiny | 18 | 20 | +8 | 1d4 + 4 |
| Small | 16 | 25 | +6 | 1d8 + 2 |
| Medium | 13 | 40 | +5 | 2d6 + 1 |
| Large | 10 | 50 | +6 | 2d10 + 2 |
| Huge | 10 | 80 | +8 | 2d12 + 4 |

**At Higher Levels:** animate **two additional** objects per slot level above 5th.

### Antilife Shell
*Abjuration · Classes: Druid* (PHB p.213)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self (10-foot radius)
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour

A shimmering barrier extends **10 feet** from you in every direction and moves with you. It prevents **creatures other than undead and constructs** from passing or reaching through it — they can't be touched by it and can't make melee attacks across it. You can still cast spells and make ranged attacks through it. If you move so that an affected creature would be forced through the barrier, the spell ends.

### Awaken
*Transmutation · Classes: Bard, Druid* (PHB p.216)

- **Casting Time:** 8 hours
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (an agate worth at least 1,000 gp, which the spell consumes)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Over 8 hours of casting, touch a **beast or plant** with an Intelligence of **3 or lower**. It gains an **Intelligence of 10**, the ability to **speak one language you know**, and (if a plant) the ability to move and senses similar to a human's. The awakened creature is **charmed by you for 30 days** (or until you or your companions harm it); afterward it chooses its own attitude.

### Banishing Smite
*Abjuration · Classes: Paladin* (PHB p.216)

- **Casting Time:** 1 bonus action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

The next time you hit a creature with a **weapon attack** before the spell ends, the attack deals an extra **5d10 force damage**. If the strike reduces the target to **50 hit points or fewer**, you **banish** it: if it's native to another plane it's sent home; otherwise it's banished to a harmless demiplane (incapacitated) until the spell ends, then it returns to the space it left (or the nearest open space).

### Bigby's Hand
*Evocation · Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard* (PHB p.218)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 120 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (an eggshell and a snakeskin glove)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

Create a **Large hand of translucent force** in range — **AC 20, hit points equal to your hit point maximum, Strength 26 (+8), Dexterity 10 (+0)**. As a **bonus action**, move it up to **60 feet** and produce one effect:
- **Clenched Fist** — melee spell attack; on a hit, **4d8 force damage**.
- **Forceful Hand** — Strength (Athletics) contest to **push** the target 5 feet (+5 feet per 5 by which you beat its check).
- **Grasping Hand** — Strength contest to **grapple** a Huge or smaller creature; while grappled, you can crush it for **2d6 bludgeoning** per turn.
- **Interposing Hand** — moves between you and a creature, giving you **half cover** and using its Strength to block the creature's path.

**At Higher Levels:** Clenched Fist damage **+2d8** and Grasping Hand crush damage **+2d6** per slot level above 5th.

### Cloudkill
*Conjuration · Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard* (PHB p.222)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 120 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes

A **20-foot-radius sphere** of poisonous, yellow-green fog appears, **heavily obscuring** its area and spreading around corners. A creature in the cloud when cast, or that **starts its turn** there, makes a **Constitution saving throw**, taking **5d8 poison damage** on a failure or half on a success. **At Higher Levels:** damage increases by 1d8 per slot level above 5th.

- **Casting Time:** 1 minute
- **Range:** Self

### Contact Other Plane
*Divination (ritual) · Classes: Warlock, Wizard* — **Ritual** (PHB p.226)

- **Casting Time:** 1 minute
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** 1 minute

Mentally contact a demigod, a long-dead sage, or another extraplanar intelligence. Make a **DC 15 Intelligence saving throw**. On a **failure**, you take **6d6 psychic damage** and are **insane** (incapacitated, unable to act) until you finish a long rest. On a **success**, you can ask up to **five questions** before the spell ends; the entity answers each in a **single word** — *yes, no, maybe, never, irrelevant,* or *unclear* — or a brief cryptic phrase. ### Contagion
*Necromancy · Classes: Cleric, Druid* (PHB p.227)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 7 days

Make a **melee spell attack**. On a hit, you afflict the creature with a **disease** of your choice (Blinding Sickness, Filth Fever, Flesh Rot, Mindfire, Seizure, or Slimy Doom). The target must make a **Constitution saving throw at the end of each of its turns**. After **three failures**, the disease takes hold for the **full duration** (and saves stop). After **three successes**, the creature **recovers** and the spell ends. Each disease has its own debilitating effects (e.g., Blinding Sickness → blinded and disadvantage on Wis checks/saves; Seizure → disadvantage on attacks, Dex checks, and Dex saves).

- **Casting Time:** 1 action

- **Components:** V, S, M (holy water or powdered silver and iron)

*Enchantment · Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Wizard* (PHB p.235)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

A **humanoid** you can see makes a **Wisdom saving throw** or is **charmed** and dominated for the duration. You have a **telepathic link** while on the same plane and can issue commands (no action). ### Geas
*Enchantment · Classes: Bard, Cleric, Druid, Paladin, Wizard* (PHB p.245)

- **Casting Time:** 1 minute
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** 30 days

A creature that can **hear and understand** you makes a **Wisdom saving throw** or becomes **charmed** and magically bound to carry out a command you give (don't issue an obviously self-destructive order). While charmed, if it acts in a way directly counter to your command, it takes **5d10 psychic damage** — at most **once each day**. The geas ends if you use an action to end it, or via *remove curse*, *greater restoration*, or *wish* (cast at 5th level).

**At Higher Levels:** with a **7th- or 8th-level** slot the duration is **1 year**; with a **9th-level** slot it lasts **until ended** by one of the above spells.

### Modify Memory
*Enchantment · Classes: Bard, Wizard* (PHB p.261)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

A creature you can see makes a **Wisdom saving throw** (it must be able to understand your language) or you can **reshape one of its memories** of an event it experienced **within the last 24 hours**, lasting no longer than 10 minutes. You can **erase** the memory, prevent it from forming, **change its details**, or **implant a new memory**. You must speak to the target to describe how its memories change; when the spell ends, it recalls the altered memory as real (though further work can be undone by *remove curse* or *greater restoration*).

**At Higher Levels:** you can target a memory up to **7 days** old (6th), **30 days** (7th), **1 year** (8th), or **any time** in the target's past (9th).

### Raise Dead
*Necromancy · Classes: Bard, Cleric, Paladin* (PHB p.270)

- **Casting Time:** 1 hour
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (a diamond worth at least 500 gp, which the spell consumes)

### Wall of Force
*Evocation · Classes: Wizard* (PHB p.285)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 120 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a pinch of powder made by crushing a clear gemstone)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes

Create an **invisible wall of force**. You can shape it as a horizontal/vertical barrier of up to **ten 10-foot-by-10-foot panels**, or as a **sphere or hemisphere up to 10 feet in radius**. The wall is **immune to damage, can't be dispelled by *dispel magic***, and **nothing can physically pass through it** (though it blocks no spells unless they require physical passage). It extends into the Ethereal Plane (blocking ethereal travel). A ***disintegrate*** spell destroys it instantly.

### Wall of Stone
*Evocation · Classes: Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard* (PHB p.287)

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 120 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a small block of granite)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes

Create a nonmagical **wall of solid stone** — up to **ten 10-foot-by-10-foot panels**, each **6 inches thick**, joined or freestanding (you can shape a bridge, a ramp, or enclose creatures, who get a **Dexterity saving throw** to escape the forming wall). Each panel has **AC 15 and 30 hit points per inch** of thickness (and is destroyed-section-by-section). If you **maintain concentration for the full 10 minutes**, the wall becomes **permanent** and can't be dispelled.
